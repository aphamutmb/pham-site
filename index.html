Dr. Anthony Pham is a board-certified medical oncologist and hematologist practicing at Compass Oncology's West Cancer Center in Tigard, Oregon. He specializes in treating all cancers with a particular emphasis on melanoma, lung cancer, and genitourinary cancers.

Dr. Pham is widely recognized for his personable nature and positive attitude. He strives to provide excellent care while developing strong relationships built on trust and compassion — ensuring that the needs of the patient always come first. He deeply understands that each patient's cancer journey is unique, and he is committed to being with them every step of the way.

During his training at the world-renowned Mayo Clinic, Dr. Pham had the opportunity to work alongside some of the foremost international experts in oncology. He brings that wealth of knowledge directly to his patients in the Pacific Northwest. He is also an active principal investigator in medical oncology research studies, giving his patients access to cutting-edge clinical trial options.

"Each patient's journey is unique, and I make sure they know I'm with them all the way — because the needs of the patient always come first."
